Explanation

Facial Expression Label Definitions:

  • 1 = Sinister
  • 2 = Sad/Sinister
  • 3 = Sad
  • 4 = Stupid
  • 5 = Stupid/Sinister

How to Use the Program:

To use this program, start by uploading images of facial expressions on the training page, and assign them labels based on the facial expression (e.g., sinister, sad, etc.). Once you have labeled enough images, you can update the model by pressing the "Update Model" button.

After updating the model, go to the classify page and upload a new image. The program will predict the facial expression based on the trained model.
